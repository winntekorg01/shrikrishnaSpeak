# Privacy Policy for KrishnaAI

**Last updated:** 19 January 2026

KrishnaAI is a conversational assistant application that provides spiritual guidance and responses through text and voice interactions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the KrishnaAI application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Account Information
When you sign in using Google authentication, we may collect:
- Your email address
- Your name
- Your profile picture (if available)

This information is used only for authentication and account identification.

### b. User Content
We collect the content you voluntarily provide while using the app, including:
- Text messages you type
- Voice inputs you record for speech-to-text processing

### c. Usage Information
We may collect limited technical and usage data such as:
- Feature usage counts (for example, trial limits)
- Timestamps related to app usage
- Error logs required for troubleshooting and security

---

## 2. Microphone and Audio Usage

KrishnaAI requests access to your device microphone **only** when you choose to use voice input features.

- Audio is used to convert speech to text and to generate spoken responses.
- KrishnaAI does **not** record audio in the background.
- Microphone access is active only during user-initiated interactions.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Authenticate users and manage accounts
- Generate text and voice responses
- Enforce trial limits and subscription entitlements
- Maintain security and prevent misuse
- Improve reliability, performance, and user experience

---

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do **not** sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with trusted service providers strictly for operating the app, such as:
- Google authentication services
- Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
- AI and speech processing service providers

These providers process data only as required to deliver their services and are expected to follow appropriate data protection practices.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to:
- Operate the application
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve technical issues and disputes

Some anonymized or minimal logs may be retained for security and diagnostic purposes.

---

## 6. Your Choices and Controls

- You may choose not to use voice features by denying microphone permission.
- You may stop using the app at any time.
- You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address below.

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
